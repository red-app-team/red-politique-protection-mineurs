Child Protection and Prevention of Sexual Abuse Policy

The Red app is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all of its users. We have a zero-tolerance policy for child exploitation and sexual abuse of minors and have implemented strict measures to prevent and report any inappropriate content or behavior.

1. Strict Prohibition of Inappropriate Content

We strictly prohibit:

  • Any content that exploits or depicts minors in an inappropriate manner.
  • Any behavior that encourages or facilitates child exploitation.
  • Any attempts at inappropriate communication with a minor through our platform.

Any such content will be immediately removed and reported to the relevant authorities.

2. Abuse Reporting Mechanism

We provide a reporting system for users to alert us to any suspicious content or behavior. Reports are handled with the utmost attention, and actions may include suspension or permanent removal of the involved account.

3. Moderation and Manual Review

Our team performs active moderation of all reported issues to ensure compliance with this policy. Any proven violations will result in immediate action.

4. Legal Compliance and Cooperation with Authorities

We comply with local and international laws regarding the protection of minors and collaborate with authorities in cases of verified abuse reports. Any illegal content will be forwarded to the appropriate agencies.
